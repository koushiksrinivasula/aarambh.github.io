About us

Actors are all about entrances, but writers are all about exits. At Aarambh, we sit on the driver's seat, journeying on the roads of theatre, to our destination - an applause by the audience.

Aarambh, combines various elements of theatre and film making, in order to make a significant impact. We've delivered excellent performances, and we'll continue to do so. We're not restricted to theatre though. All world's a stage for us. Alas, great drama is about challenging how we think and encouraging us to fantasize about a world we aspire to.
